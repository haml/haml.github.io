Haml has a thriving community that's eager to help out. There are several places where you can go to ask questions or receive debugging assistance. Before you do, though, make sure to check the FAQs.

  • Norman is on Twitter as @compay and usually responds to questions very quickly.
  • The Haml IRC channel, #haml on irc.freenode.net, is a great place to ask questions.
  • If you have a longer question (or no one’s around on IRC at the moment), the Haml Google Group is a great place to go. You’ll usually get a response within a day or so — sooner, if the developers are paying attention when you send it.
